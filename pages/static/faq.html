Frequently Asked Questions

What makes DeViine unique?

DeViine.com is a web app for recreational cannabis connoisseurs to easily rate and review strains, concentrates, edibles and dispensaries to remember their experiences with each. Modeled as the “Amazon Reviews for Recreational Cannabis”, DeViine is recreational-only, so us recreational users don’t have to sift through unrelevant medicinal information. User experience and design is as the top of our priorities, so you can rest assured knowing our information will be easy-to-use and palatable.

How does it work?

What is a Processor?

Recreational cannabis processors are licensed by the Washington State Liquor Control Board to trim, package and / or prepare the product for consumption by consumers. Processors can concentrate cannabis into oil, wax, shatter, edibles, beverages, etc. Processors can also be producers.

What is a Producer?

Recreational cannabis producers are licensed by the Washington State Liquor Control Board to grow cannabis. Producers can also be processors.

Can I leave ratings or reviews without an account?

No. You may visit the site as a guest, but in order to contribute to our community and track your favorites, you must first SIGN UP for a FREE account. We strongly suggest you make an account and check out the perks of being a user, such as saving your favorite strains, concentrates, edibles and dispensaries. You can always sign in to remember your previous experiences with each product or store.

Something doesn’t seem to be working correctly, what do I do?

Because of the amount of internet devices and browsers available, we cannot always fully support all of them perfectly. If you come across a bug or something not working correctly, please let us know HERE. We will do our best to get the bugs fixed as quickly as possible.

How can I get my products listed?

If you are a Washington State Liquor Control Board licensed recreational cannabis producer or processor, tell us a little about your product HERE. We’ll be in touch. If you are a licensed business in Alaska, Colorado, Oregon or Washington D.C., send us an email to support@deviine.com. We’ll let you know when you can expect to get your products on DeViine.

How can my business become a sponsor?

Our sponsors have either donated giveaway items or have helped us along our journey in some way. Interested in becoming a sponsor? Send us an email to support@deviine.com and we’ll see what we can do for you!
